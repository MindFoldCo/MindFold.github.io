Pricing FAQs

Is the free plan really free forever? +
Yes, genuinely. The Starter plan is free for up to 3 users with no time limit. No credit card required to sign up.
Can I switch plans at any time? +
Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. If you downgrade mid-cycle, we'll prorate your bill automatically.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), and PayPal. Enterprise customers can also pay via invoice.
How does the 14-day trial work? +
When you sign up, you automatically get a full 14-day trial of the Growth plan — no credit card needed. At the end of the trial, you can choose to upgrade or stay on the free Starter plan.
Is there a discount for non-profits or education? +
Yes! We offer a 50% discount for registered non-profits and educational institutions. Reach out to our team at team@mindfold.co to apply.
