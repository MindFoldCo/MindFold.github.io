Common questions

How long does setup really take? +
Most teams are fully set up in under 10 minutes. If you're importing from another tool, it typically takes a few minutes more depending on the size of your data.
Do I need to train my team to use MindFold? +
Nope. MindFold is built to be intuitive from day one. Most team members figure it out in their first session without any formal training — though we have docs and walkthroughs if you want them.
Can I migrate from Trello, Jira, or Asana? +
Yes — we have one-click importers for Trello, Jira, Asana, and Notion. Your tasks, assignees, and due dates all come across cleanly.
What if my team works across different time zones? +
MindFold handles this gracefully. Each person's daily plan adjusts to their local timezone, and async commenting means no one needs to be online at the same time to stay in sync.
Is there a limit on how many projects I can create? +
On the free Starter plan, you get 5 active projects. On Growth and Enterprise plans, projects are unlimited.
